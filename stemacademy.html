STEM Academy

Centro Cultural y Juvenil de Tecnología y Robótica Educativa Aplicada.

In our mission to bring robotics and educational technology throughout the country, with special emphasis on schools and public high schools, our team gives talks showing our work, projects and, of course, our robots. In addition we participate in national robotics competitions and we carry out activities for the dissemination of STEM, through our non-profit organization STEM Academy Chile, which was created as a formal institution towards channeling our efforts to increase our reach. Indeed, Pizza Mecánica (FRC team 5512) is one of its lines of work.

Science Fairs

Each year out team takes part at science fairs in our community to talk about our work and what is FIRST. We also go to school to showcase our robots and empower other kids. The bigger science fair in the city is organized in the city hall, where we have showed our FRC robot in the council room.

#YoPuedoProgramar | The Hour of Code

The first nation campaign of the Hour of Code was done in 2015. Since then, every year as STEM Academy, we have run 1 Hour of Code per day, 1 school a day for the week of the campaign. On 2016, we also collaborated with Microsoft to widen our reach and took part in #YoPuedoProgramar. Similar with Microssoft's mission, we seek to empower every student to do more.

Educational Robotics & VEX Tournaments

We have a team of middle and elementary schools students and a high school one that competes in Chile's VEX tournaments. We have mentored FLL teams and we also go to schools to do workshops with our robots. Our ultimate goal is to start 1 team or STEM-oriented community in each of the 19 public school of our city, where the opportunity would be almost non-existent otherwise.

Laboratorios Tecnológicos (Tech Laboratories)

We call Teach Laboratories, to a serie of workshops we do through our educational tool called "Firstmakers". It is an adapted Arduino Uno with incorporated sensors and easy-to-use components which helps us teach 10+ year olds learn about programming and electronics. With it they can build robots, automatic irrigation systems and alarms, without the need to weld and also know programming.

Pizza Mecánica | FRC team

Between January and February, in a period of 6 weeks, we come together as Pizza Mecánica, FRC team 5512 to conceive, build and program a robots that will travel all the way from Chile to the US!.
