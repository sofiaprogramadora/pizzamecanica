About Us

Founded by the end of 2014, Team 5512 is based one of Chile’s oldest and emblematic Public Schools, Liceo Valentín Letelier. Our team is composed by high school students helped by volunteer mentors who participate and represent our country in the international FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC), recognized as one of the most complex robotics tournament for high school students in the world. We are one of only two Chilean teams in the competition, and this year, the only Latin American team competing in Los Angeles Regional Event in California, which takes place between March 23 and 26 of this year.
