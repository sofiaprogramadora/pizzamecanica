Our Community

Team 5512 is strongly aware of the power of giving back to the community. Let us explain a little more about it.

Our team is born at the end of 2014 at the eave of the Robotics Workshop of the Liceo Valentín Letelier (our high school), in Recoleta. With experience in FIRST LEGO League and VEX Robotics Competition, our members decided to take the "big step", becoming the only FRC team based in a Public / Municipal school across the country.

Recoleta

Recoleta is a city located in the northern sector of the city of Santiago, capital of Chile. It has been recognized as the most transparent locality of Chile, and despite its low schooling and income indices compared to surrounding localitties, Recoleta is famous for being a successful case of immigrant inclusion, making diagnoses and better action plans, examples to incorporate immigrants into Chilean society. In the city live approximately 60.000 kids and young people bteween 7 and 29. Besides, it has 19 public schools and high schools under its municipal administration, one of them being our high school, the Liceo Valentín Letelier.

Valentín Letelier High Schoool

The "Liceo Valentin Letelier" (our high school) is one of the most emblematic establishments in Chile. It was founded in 1888 and began its educational activities in March 1889, in an old house that had been owned by Diego Portales, a Minister of State, recognized as one of the fundamental figures of the consolidation of the State of Chile. Nowadays, both our city and our high school has a high number of immigrant students mostly from low income households, which supports our diversity and strengthen our willing to do more and doubles our efforts t spread our work.
