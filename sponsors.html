About Sponsorship | Our Sponsors

Our largest and most loyal sponsor is the Municipality of Recoleta, headed by its Mayor Mr. Daniel Jadue. The Recoleta Municipality, in its commitment to strengthening Public Education, sees in us a model initiative in which Chilean students, regardless of their origin, develop complex models using cutting-edge technology and competing at the international level in the United States.

In the same way as various institutions that support our work, such as Microsoft Chile, U.S. Embassy Santiago, Robotics Lab SCL, Prosegur Foundation, among others.
